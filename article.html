Experiences : Adventure in Prau
" Experiences the journey to see the most fascinating places in Java"
  Art experience by Yoda
   September,27th 2017

Prau is a mountain located in Center of Java Island and its directly located in Dieng Plateu.For people who like nature, I would recommend spend a night in Mt Prau. I did a solo trip in Sept'16 and did not regret it at all, if that is the only mountain in Indonesia, maybe I would definitely come back, but the experience makes me wanting to explore other mountains in Indonesia. Imagine, woke up with millions of stars above you and the ray of sunrise slowly emerging into the sky, and you can see cloud beneath you? It is just magnificent!
